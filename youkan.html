What is YouKan?

YouKan is a simple Kanban app that replaces your Chrome new tab page. With an easy drag-and-drop interface, it helps you manage tasks—whether for work or personal use—without any distractions.

Features

  • Drag-and-drop task management
  • Lightweight and minimal interface
  • Integrated with your Chrome new tab
  • No account or login required
  • Easy backup and restore
